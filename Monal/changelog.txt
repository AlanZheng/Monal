
Monal is an XMPP/Jabber IM client.  It can  connect to any standard  XMPP  chat server such as  Google talk or facebook chat.
Notable features:

★ Free and No Ads!
★ Strong focus on privacy. Does not phone home and does not have "metrics" software. 
★ A direct client and does not transmit your password to a 3rd party.
★ Standard XMPP Jingle VOIP. Allows voice conversation with any other user with a program that supports it (Google Talk)
★ Will work with corporate XMPP servers that require VPN
★ MUC multi user chat
★ Tested heavily with google talk, facebook, openfire, prosody   and ejabberd servers.
